Band Overview

Queen are a British rock band formed in London in 1970. Their classic line-up was Freddie Mercury, Brian May, Roger Taylor and John Deacon.They sold over 300 million records worldwide and a lot of their songs are still popular today.

Band Members

  • Freddy Mercury (Lead Singer and piano)
  • Brian May (Guitar)
  • Roger Taylor (Guitar)
  • John Deacon (Bass Guitar)

Top 5 Popular Songs by Queen

(According to Rolling Stone Poll)

  1. "Bohemian Rhapsody"
  2. "Fat Bottomed Girls"
  3. "Don't Stop Me Now"
  4. "Somebody to Love"
  5. "Who Wants to Live Forever"
